Hello.

Photosynthetic Arts is a collective of artists based on photography, videography and webdesign. We are focusing on landscape-, nature-, lifestyle- and eventphotography.

Beside the passion for cameras, we are connected through the love to nature, our lifestyle, travelling and nevertheless the desire to evolve as conscious human being. This was the headstone to pave the way for a collective cooperation.

We have discovered creative spirits in our beloved sphere and their productions laid the base for our first projects as a collective. Stay tuned!

We want to create. We want to dream. We want to be inspired. We want to inspire. Lets get in touch!

With Passion - With Love ❤️
